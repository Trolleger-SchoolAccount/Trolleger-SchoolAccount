Quick Tortellini Recipe

This recipe is for a simple, delicious, and quick tortellini dish thatâ€™s perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • Tortellini (preferably meat or spinach)
  • 1-2 eggs
  • Cooking cream
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Pepper (to taste)

Directions

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil over high heat.
  2. Once boiling, add salt to the water.
  3. Add the tortellini and cook for 3 minutes.
  4. Drain the water while keeping the tortellini in the pot.
  5. Add cooking cream and mix over medium heat until slightly reduced and creamy.
  6. Turn off the heat and add 1 or 2 eggs, mixing them in and letting them cook using the residual heat.
  7. Serve on a plate. Season with pepper and more salt if needed, and optionally top with shredded cheese.

Enjoy your creamy tortellini!
